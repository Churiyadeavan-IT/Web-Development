How to play:

  • Blackjack, also known as "21", is a card game where players try to get a higher hand value without going over 21.
  • The value of Number cards is the corresponding number in the card, the value of Face cards is 10, and the value of Ace card is 1 or 11 (can be determined according to the best for the player's game)
  • Player's sum is calculated by the value of the cards they have.
  • In the beginning, the players can draw two cards, but in further rounds they can draw one card each round.
  • If sum of cards the player have is less than 21, then they can draw another card in the next round.
  • If the sum of cards is equals to 21, the player wins.
  • If the sum of cards exceeds 21, then player lose.
